Gas Price Across the World

Visualization by Jeff Chong Di Wei

In late December 2019, Covid-19 emerged from Wuhan, China, and resulted in a formidable outbreak in many cities in China and expanded globally.
One of the impacts of Covid-19 is the sharp drop in gas prices.
After a few years, many of the countries have adapted to the pandemic and the gas prices started to recover back to the normal states.
We will be visualizing the gas prices around the world on 23rd June 2022.

What was the Gas Price on 23rd June 2022?

From the map, we can see that the price of gas in the countries of Oceania such as
Tonga and Niue are more expensive compared to countries in Asia such as Saudi Arabia.
This is because Asia has more oil reserves in the world.

Country that has the most expensive gas price:

1. Tonga: 16.2USD

2. Niue: 11.43USD

3. Hong Kong: 11.35USD

Country that has the cheapest gas price:
